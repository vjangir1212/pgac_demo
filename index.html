About PGAC

The Post Graduate Academic Council (PGAC) is the official student body representing the academic concerns of postgraduate students. Formed in 2009, it serves as a bridge between IIT Bombay's administration and the PG community.Headed by General Secretary of Academic Affairs (PG).
